Meet the Team

At Hot Beans, we’re a passionate group of developers, designers, and digital thinkers who turn bold ideas into powerful websites. We believe in creativity, collaboration, and crafting web experiences that truly stand out. Get to know the people behind the code!

Emily Carter — Lead Web Developer

Emily is the creative powerhouse behind Hot Beans’ major web projects. With over eight years in the industry, she specializes in building fast, functional, and beautifully designed websites. Emily loves solving complex problems with clean code and mentoring junior developers. Her motto? “Simple is better than complicated.

Marcus Lee — UX/UI Designer

Marcus makes sure every Hot Beans website looks and feels incredible. With a background in digital design and psychology, he creates user experiences that are both beautiful and intuitive. Marcus is passionate about sleek visuals, smooth journeys, and staying ahead of design trends.

Priya keeps Hot Beans’ projects on track and running smoothly. She coordinates between clients, developers, and designers, ensuring timelines, budgets, and expectations are managed perfectly. Priya’s positive energy and sharp attention to detail make every project a success.
